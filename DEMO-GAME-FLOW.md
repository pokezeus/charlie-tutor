### Step 3: Multiplication Bingo Game
**What you see:**
- 3×3 grid with random numbers (e.g., 12, 45, 67, 23, 89, 34, 56, 78, 9)
- Timer: 60 seconds countdown
- Score: 0
- Question displayed: "7 × 8 = ?"

**Gameplay:**
1. Calculate: 7 × 8 = 56
2. Find and click "56" in the grid
3. If correct → Score increases, new question appears
4. If wrong → Try again

**Sample Questions:**
- 7 × 8 = 56 ✓
- 9 × 6 = 54 ✓
- 12 × 5 = 60 ✓
- 8 × 8 = 64 ✓
- 7 × 9 = 63 ✓

**Game End:**
- Timer runs out OR all questions answered
- Final score displayed: "4/5 correct (80%)"
- Coins earned: 40 💰
- XP earned: 40 ⭐
- Button: "Back to Dashboard"
